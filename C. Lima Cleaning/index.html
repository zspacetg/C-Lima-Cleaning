About us

from Brazil to you

At C. Lima Cleaning, we take immense pride in our commitment to providing top-notch cleaning services to the vibrant community of Atlanta. Founded by a Brazilian entrepreneur with a passion for dedication to exceptional service, our company has quickly become a trusted name in the local cleaning industry.


Our journey began when our founder, Cristina, brought her experience and expertise from Brazil to the bustling city of Atlanta. With a strong desire to offer the same level of cleanliness and attention to detail that she had seen back home, she embarked on a mission to create a cleaning company that sets a new standard in quality and professionalism.
